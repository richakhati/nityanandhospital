About Us


Nityanand ICCU & Nursing Home was established on December 6th, 1992 by founding partners – Dr Harish Bhatt & Dr Harish Shetty. Initially it was started with OPD, IPD, ICCU and 24 hour emergency care. We had the aim of providing optimal yet affordable health care facilities including ICCU to the local community of Vile Parle & Andheri.

Since its inception we are treating over 10,000 out-patients, 1200 in-patients and 250 critical care cases annually. We have grown not just in mere size and patient bed capacity but also in level of quality of care. We have emerged to be one of the leading mid-size health care providers in Western Mumbai.

For nearly 3 decades, we have been forerunners in providing the best quality of medical care to the resident population. The fact that a sizeable proportion of cases continue to be on our services inspite of migrating to distant locations is a testament of the warm services provided by our entire team.

Since 2018, we have introduced Cancer & Otorhinolaryngology (ENT) services in our hospital. With entry of young capable professionals, we now look forward to serving the population in Cancer – whose incidence is rising at an alarming rate and ENT – a speciality of its own which is gaining increasing focus.

With widening horizons and newer experts in our team, our dream continues to remain one – Quality Health Care at Affordable Prices.

Jai Hind !!

Mission Statement

We are dedicated to consistently enrich the quality of life of all our patients and visitors by providing excellent and accessible health services to all in the need of care in a diverse urban community regardless of the status and ability to pay. We are devoted to providing a resource for compassionate speciality and super speciality services; and to be innovators in integrated health facilities. We are committed to develop co-operative relationships with other healthcare providers and promotion of wellness through health education and awareness.

Vision

Delivering comprehensive yet optimal care to all individuals, taking particular consideration of the needs of the vulnerable population in an ethically and financially responsible manner.

To promote healthful habits and in turn a happy society.

Core Values

Our team is like a family upholding highest regard for dignity, integrity and diversity. We reflect these core values in our daily behaviour and work practices:

  • Respect: We serve all individuals of the populace with highest esteem. We honour the efforts of all our team members.
  • Professionalism: We apply a high degree of medical, nursing and technical competence. We strive for excellence in optimal standards of care, safety and service by adopting innovation and persistent improvement. We trust each other and communicate authentically.
  • Diversity: We respect the dynamic nature of our society and we honour their ethnic, religious and cultural differences.
  • Compassion: We empathize with the needs of others.
  • Ethics: We demonstrate integrity and loyalty with highest regard for confidentiality. We hold ourselves and others accountable.
  • Cost-effectiveness: We promote affordable, responsible and high quality health care.
