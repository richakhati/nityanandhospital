Cardiology

The department of Cardiology offers specialized and advanced treatment strategies. Our integrated amenities are supported by state-of-the-art cardiac care for any heart ailment seconded by superlative ICCU management. The department has earned a lot of good will to its name for saving the lives of many patients. We are known to be the first outreach health care facility in Vile Parle & Andheri for cases of heart attacks & stroke (paralysis).
